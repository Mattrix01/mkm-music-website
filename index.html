About Me

I am an experienced producer and mixing engineer with over 11 years experience in the industry. I have had the privilege to work on projects all over London including Abbey Road and Metropolis. I have worked with hundreds of clients from across the globe, including Xfactor star Ryan Lawrie, The Voice finalist and winners Clara Hurardo and Jessica Hammond, Britians got talents Henrietta Adele, Nottinghill Music's songwriter Joe Killington, International music icon Rahat Fateh Ali Kahn, as well as having a number 1 in the iTunes World Album Charts with IM1313 to name a few of my achievments.

I have produced, mixed and mastered everything from modern electronic and urban music to film and live recorded sound tracks to visual art or animation which has had radio play as well as chart presence. I have used my experience to get my clients music where it needs to be. I have both a versatile, innovative and proactive approach to all my projects which make me a great choice for your music production and engineering needs.
